Decadent Chocolate Brownies

Description

Prepare to indulge in pure chocolate bliss with these decadent brownies, where rich cocoa meets velvety smoothness in every bite. With a perfectly crackly crust and a fudgy interior, these brownies are a chocolate lover's dream come true, guaranteed to leave you swooning with delight and reaching for seconds.

Here's what you'll need:

  • Butter
  • Granulated sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • All-purpose flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Chocolate chips (optional)

Steps to chocolate heaven:

  1. Melt butter and mix with sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  2. Whisk dry ingredients together; fold into wet mixture.
  3. Add chocolate chips, if desired.
  4. Pour batter into a prepared pan; bake until set.
  5. Cool, slice, and devour with abandon!
