Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Embark on a journey to Italy with this classic Spaghetti Bolognese, where rich tomato sauce meets perfectly cooked pasta, creating a symphony of flavors that will transport you to the streets of Bologna. With each bite, savor the harmonious blend of savory ground beef, aromatic herbs, and tender spaghetti, guaranteed to tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

Here are the ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Onion, carrot, and celery
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Garlic
  • Red wine (optional)
  • Spices and herbs
  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
